FAQ


How to register for courses?

You can register for courses through the Courses section. Look for the desired course and click on the "Register" button.

How to cancel registeration of the course?

To cancel your registration, go to the Courses section, find the course you want to cancel, and click on the "Cancel Registration" button.

Where can I see which courses are coming in the following years?

You can View upcoming courses in the ISP section at the bottom of the page.

Where can I see what grades I got?

You can see your grades in the Completed studies section by clicking on the year it was on and dropdown menu will show it.

Where to download study transcript?

You can download your transcript of records in PDF format from the Completed studies section by clicking on the "Download" button.
