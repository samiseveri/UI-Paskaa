Info

In here you will find info on how to use Pakki and what each section has to offer.


ISP / Individual student plans

In ISP you can see how many credits you have completed and how many you still need to complete at least to get your degree.
You can see how many credits you have completed each year and switch between different study years from the bottom of the page.
There you can see what courses are coming up in the next years so you can plan your studies better if you want to do some specific courses.

Courses

In Courses you can all the current courses and coming up courses.
You can see the courses that have open registeration.
You can register and cancel your registeration for courses in this section.

Completer studies

In Completed studies you can see all the courses you have completed so far and the grades you got in those courses.
You can see the courses sorted by the study year you completed them in and see all the courses in one list.
You can download a transcript of records in PDF format from this section.
Click the dropdown menus to see everything since these are collapsed by default.
You can filter the courses by different criteria using the filter options on each dropdown menu such as if it was a mandatory or optional course.

Calendar

In Calendar you can see your schedule for each week.
Times, locations and other details of your courses are shown.

Contact

In Contact you can find contact informations of teachers of TurkuAMK.
You can see first your own course teachers and below that all of the teachers where you can search for a specific teacher using the search bar.

FAQ

In FAQ you can find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Pakki.
